Used also as signs chars format AG85UASCF1
By Arthur Golubev 19850316
2025-08-22
Used also as signs chars format AG85ASCF1 is applicable for texts that can’t contain successive sequence of the signs, and is as follows:
1. Single occurrence of a char for which there is a corresponded sign means the sign occurrence in the place of the text;
2. A successive sequence of a char for which there is a corresponded sign means successive occurrences of the char with length less by one than length of the successive sequence in the place of the text.
